About Us

Aviator Realty is a full-service real estate brokerage focusing on residential purchases and sales. David Rosenstein founded the agency in 2019 after working in the DFW market as a realtor for six years. The agency has been steadily growing and now has agents that specialize in all areas of the DFW metroplex.

Whether you’re a first time home buyer, or a seasoned homeowner, our goal is to guide you through the home buying or selling process and make the experience as smooth as possible.

Have questions or want to know more about how we can help? Give us a call at (817) 458-8080 or send us a note at info@aviatorrealty.com.
